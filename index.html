who are the arctic monkeys?

Arctic Monkeys is a British rock band formed in 2002 in Sheffield, England. The band consists of four members: Alex Turner (lead vocals, guitar), Jamie Cook (guitar, backing vocals), Matt Helders (drums), and Nick O'Malley (bass). They are known for their energetic performances and clever lyricism, with their debut album "Whatever People Say I Am, That's What I'm Not" being the fastest-selling debut album in UK chart history. The band has gained worldwide recognition and has been influential in the indie rock scene.
